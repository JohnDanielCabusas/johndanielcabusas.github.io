About Us

LinTech is a company of reconditioned tech products, dedicated to offering outstanding value and sustainability. The business specializes in bringing used Electronic devices with specific problems making it brand new while making sure it adhere to strict quality and performance requirements which is a great way to help people all around especially people who are facing financial struggles. This business specifically targets individuals and families experiencing financial hardship by offering affordable options for refurbishing Electronic devices. The products are reliable, the services are optimal, making it budget-friendly to battle the E-waste that is one of the rising problems in not just our country but to many as well.
